Smart queries, smarter investments

  • ? How do you help with the claim process in case of a health or life insurance claim?

    We assist you at every step, from documentation to claim submission and follow-ups with the insurer. We ensure your claim is processed smoothly and efficiently.

  • ? What documents will you need from my family for a claim settlement?

    Typically, we need a claim form, policy document, ID proof of the claimant, death certificate (for life insurance), hospitalization bills and reports (for health insurance), and any additional documents required by the insurer.

  • ? How can I claim for my annual health check-up under my insurance policy?

    To claim a health check-up, please provide the original bills, test reports, and a filled claim form. We’ll assist you in submitting it to the insurer for reimbursement.

  • ? Will EIGN support me during hospitalization?

    Absolutely! We assist with pre-authorization for cashless hospitalization, documentation support with the hospital’s TPA (Third Party Administrator) to ensure a smooth experience.

  • ? Does my policy cover pre- and post-hospitalization expenses?

    Most health insurance policies cover medical expenses incurred 30 days before and 60-90 days after hospitalization, but it depends on the policy terms. We’ll confirm the coverage in your specific policy and help with claims.

  • ? How can EIGN assist during an emergency hospitalization?

    During an emergency, contact us immediately. We’ll help with cashless hospitalization (if available), coordinate with the insurer/TPA, and guide you or your family through the process.

  • ? How can I get the list of cashless hospitals under my policy?

    We’ll provide you with an updated network hospital list, and we can also assist you in finding the nearest cashless hospital when needed.

  • ? Does EIGN support cashless repairs at garages for my vehicle insurance?

    Yes! We can provide you with the list of cashless garages authorized by your vehicle insurance company and assist you with the claim process.

  • ? Does EIGN provide emergency support at any time via email or phone?

    Yes, EIGN provides 24x7 support via email (support@eign.com) and a dedicated helpline to assist you with any queries or emergencies.
