EFFICIENT RENEWAL PROCESS

“Secure your dreams by renewing your commitments"

Eign’s customized application features a renewal dashboard where clients can view upcoming renewals and review their investment and insurance details. Clients can also renew policies directly through the application at any time.

Notifications and Alerts:

Implement automated reminders for clients with email and SMS notifications starts a month before renewal due dates. Alerts will cover policy renewals, SIP payments, and upcoming expirations. Additionally, a dedicated support team reviews the client’s financial assets in advance to ensure timely completion of renewals.

Ensuring Timely Payments

Direct Debit Setup: For mutual funds and insurance premiums, encourage clients to set up auto-debit mandates (ECS/NACH) to ensure timely payments and prevent policy lapses or SIP interruptions.

Documentation and Record-Keeping:

Transaction Records: Maintain detailed records of all renewal payments and related transactions.

Policy Documentation:Ensure that all policy documents and renewal confirmations are accurate and easily accessible.

Error Handling and Dispute Resolution:

Issue Management: Implement a system for handling payment errors or disputes, providing clients with clear instructions on how to resolve issues.

Support Access: Offer prompt customer assistance support for any payment-related inquiries.

Smart queries, smarter investments

  • 01 What is the process to renew a lapsed policy, and are there any penalties or medical tests required?

    To renew a lapsed policy, you generally need to pay the outstanding premium plus interest (if any). Some policies may require a health declaration or medical test. We’ll assist you with the specific process depending on the insurer.

  • 02 How much time do I have to renew a lapsed health policy before it gets terminated?

    Usually, you have a grace period of 30-90 days after the due date (depending on the insurer). After this period, the policy may terminate, so we recommend acting quickly.

  • 03 How will you remind me of policy renewals?

    We send renewal reminders via SMS, email, WhatsApp, and phone calls to ensure you don’t miss any important dates.

  • 04 Can I receive reminders via SMS, email, or WhatsApp?

    Yes! You can choose your preferred communication channel, and we’ll send reminders accordingly.

  • 05 Can you explain the key features and benefits of my insurance policy?

    Of course! We’ll provide a detailed explanation of the sum insured, premium details, inclusions, exclusions, riders, claim process, and other important features specific to your policy.

  • 06 Are there any riders or add-ons I can include for better coverage?

    Yes, many policies offer riders such as accidental death cover, critical illness cover, or hospital cash benefits.
