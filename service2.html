Smart queries, smarter investments

  • 01 How can I update my nominee details on my existing policies?

    Simply contact us with your request, and we will guide you through the endorsement process.

  • 02 What documents do I need to submit to process an endorsement or nominee change?

    Typically, you need to submit a duly filled Nominee Change Form, ID proof of the nominee (like Aadhaar or PAN), relationship proof (if applicable), and a copy of the existing policy document.

  • 03 Does my current policy cover consumable expenses (like PPE kits, gloves, etc.)?

    Most standard policies exclude consumables, but some plans offer optional riders for these expenses. We’ll review your policy and suggest if you need additional coverage.

  • 04 Can I upgrade my room category during hospitalization?

    Yes, but it’s subject to the insurer’s terms. Some policies limit reimbursement based on your eligible room rent. We’ll help you understand the additional cost implications and coordinate with the insurer.
